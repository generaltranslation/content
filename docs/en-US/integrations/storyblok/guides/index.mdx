Use these guides to connect a Storyblok space and run translation workflows with General Translation.

- [Configure Storyblok](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/configure-storyblok): Connect a Storyblok space to General Translation with credentials, and enable translatable fields.
- [Translate content](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/translate-content): Translate Storyblok stories from the General Translation Dashboard and review the results summary.
- [Manage translations](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/manage-translations): Re-sync Storyblok stories with General Translation, reuse existing translations, and review draft-only writes.
- [Use translated content](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/use-translated-content): Learn how General Translation stores Storyblok translations per language and how to fetch them from the delivery API.
