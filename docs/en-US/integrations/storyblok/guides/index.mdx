Use these guides to connect a Storyblok space and run translation workflows with General Translation.

- [Configuring Storyblok](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/configuring-storyblok): Connect a Storyblok space to General Translation with credentials, and enable translatable fields.
- [Translating content](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/translating-content): Translate Storyblok stories from the General Translation Dashboard and review the results summary.
- [Managing translations](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/managing-translations): Re-sync Storyblok stories with General Translation, reuse existing translations, and review draft-only writes.
- [Using translated content](/docs/integrations/storyblok/guides/using-translated-content): Learn how General Translation stores Storyblok translations per language and how to fetch them from the delivery API.
