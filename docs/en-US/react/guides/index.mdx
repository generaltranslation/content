Browse how-to Guides for General Translation across React, Next.js, TanStack Start, and React Native. Each guide covers all four frameworks and shows per-framework differences in tabs.

- [Configuring General Translation](/docs/react/guides/configuring): Set up initialization, the provider, credentials, and translation delivery for your framework.
- [Translating JSX](/docs/react/guides/translating-jsx): Translate JSX content in place with the General Translation [`<T>`](/docs/react/reference/components/t) component.
- [Translating strings](/docs/react/guides/translating-strings): Translate standalone strings such as labels and placeholders with the [`useGT`](/docs/react/reference/hooks/use-gt) hook and Next.js [`getGT`](/docs/node/reference/functions/get-gt).
- [Formatting numbers and dates](/docs/react/guides/formatting-variables): Insert locale-aware numbers, currencies, dates, and variables into translated content.
- [Handling plurals and branches](/docs/react/guides/handling-plurals-and-branches): Render count-based and conditional content with the [`<Plural>`](/docs/react/reference/components/plural) and [`<Branch>`](/docs/react/reference/components/branch) components.
- [Managing locales](/docs/react/guides/managing-locales): Let users switch languages and read the active locale.
- [Translating with dictionaries](/docs/react/guides/translating-with-dictionaries): Translate strings from a central dictionary with the [`useTranslations`](/docs/react/reference/hooks/use-translations) hook.
- [Storing translations locally](/docs/react/guides/storing-translations): Bundle translations in your app instead of loading them from the General Translation CDN.
- [Linting your code](/docs/react/guides/linting-your-code): Catch common internationalization mistakes with the General Translation ESLint plugin.
