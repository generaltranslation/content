Archie McKenzie is the founder and CEO of General Translation, Inc., a company dedicated to helping developers ship multilingual software.
With an international background spanning Bath, England, Kenya, and New Zealand, Archie brings a global perspective to his work.

He holds a degree in Computer Science from Princeton University,
where he also served as a Teaching Assistant for renowned computer scientist Brian Kernighan from 2021 to 2024.

Passionate about breaking down language barriers in technology, Archie leads General Translation's mission to make software more accessible across different languages and cultures.
